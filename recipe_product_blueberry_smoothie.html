Blueberry & Apple Smoothies

Add Recipe after choosing your package
5 min.

  • 1dl Apple Juice
  • 100g Blueberries
  • 2dl Almond Milk
  • 1dl Vanilla Yoghurt
  • Freshly cut Apple

    • Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Serve in a Nice glass with a straw and garnished with a fresh berry or thin apple slice.
